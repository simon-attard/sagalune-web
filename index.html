What Sagalune is

A knowledgeable friend in the passenger seat.

Sagalune narrates the history, nature, and hidden stories of the places you pass — generated live as you drive. No pre-planned routes. No dead zones. Just stories, on any road, in any country.

01 / Stories

Stories, not tours.

Not a pre-recorded audio tour. Every narration is generated fresh for where you actually are, right now.

02 / Coverage

Any road, any country.

Motorways, back roads, and countryside you've never heard of. The app goes where you go.

03 / Hands-free

Hands-free by design.

Built for driving. Your eyes stay on the road. Your ears get the story.

How it works

Three things happen as you drive.

I.
You set off.
Tap start. Pop the phone in the cradle. Sagalune rides along quietly.
II.
A place finds you.
As you pass a village, a ruin, a river — Sagalune notices and writes something worth hearing.
III.
The story plays.
A warm voice tells you something specific — a date, a legend, a detail nobody mentions. Then it's quiet again.
